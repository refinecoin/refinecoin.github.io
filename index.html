DECENTRALIZED SOCIAL NETWORK

Refine uses tokens as a way to incentivize factual social media coverage. With no central authority, the site is curated by the people who use it; making it much harder for biased social media. Everything is fact checked by reviewers who are paid in tokens for checking. Writers are rewarded by the readers by the quality of content, not what the media agenda decides to push as the trending media.
